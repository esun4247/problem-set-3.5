Chess is Fun

Chess Club can be enjoyable for anyone. Chess is an actually interesting game, and it's very fun to play when you learn the rules. Chess club can be a great place to just enjoy yourself and learn a new skill. There are plenty of other students there that can help you practice and continue to hone your skills at the game. Experiment with different ways to play, such as putting a time limit on turns, or forcing players to switch off. There are a number of different fun ways that chess can be played, and Chess Club is the perfect place to play them. All that we ask is that you play at least a couple games when you show up.
