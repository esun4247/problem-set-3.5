You Don't Need Any Experience to Join Chess Club

Inexperienced? No Problem!

There are a number of things for players of any skill level here at Chess Club. Whether you are looking for some instructions on how to improve yourself, or your already confident in your skills, there are a number of activities for you! See the table below for more details, or talk to a member for more information.


Experience Level Activity
Beginner Learn how to play from a more experienced player or friend
Moderately Experienced Play with friends and gradually get more skilled
Expert Play at one of the competitions
